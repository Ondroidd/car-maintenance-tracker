# car-maintenance-tracker
track your car's services (oil change, tires, inspection, etc.) and fuel economy

 1. Maintenance log - track services performed (oil change, tires, inspection, etc.)
	- Date, mileage, service type, cost, notes
 2. Fuel tracking - log fill-ups and calculate fuel economy
	- Date, mileage, liters, cost, price per liter
 3. Simple reporting - view history and basic stats
